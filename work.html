Meet App

Meet App is a progressive web app that lets you search for tech events in your city. You can see how many events are happening in a particular city and time-frame, the details of those events, and what types of events are most common in an area. It's connected to Google Calendar for demonstration purposes, so the events aren't up-to-date.
